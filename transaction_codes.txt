## Notes:
1. Transaction codes may vary based on SAP version (ECC, S/4HANA)
2. Some transactions require specific authorizations
3. Custom Z* transactions may exist in your system
4. Always test transactions in development environment first
5. Use transaction /n to end current transaction
6. Use transaction /o to open new session
